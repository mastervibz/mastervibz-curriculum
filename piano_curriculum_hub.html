Three principles running through all six stages
01
Ear leads hand. Always. If the student cannot sing what they are about to play, they are not ready to play it.
02
Slow is not boring — slow is accurate. Tempo is the last variable to increase, not the first.
03
Every session contains something old (consolidation), something current (focus), and something new (appetite).
